  • 2005年暑假我在研究資訊教育能力指標時，除了看美國聯邦政府教育部的「國家教育科技標準」之外，我發現俄亥俄州政府教育部所頒布的科技課程標準(Ohio technology academic content standards)的內容，其中的「分年能力指標」最能為教學現場老師所用，我 很想翻譯出來給大家參考，不過由於時間限制只能一點一點的來，盼望有興趣的老師加入翻譯的行列。
     
  • 俄亥俄州科技課程標準 (standards)共有七個
    1. Nature of Technology
      Students develop an understanding of technology, its characteristics, scope,
      core concepts* and relationships between technologies and other fields.
       
    2. Technology and Society Interaction
      Students recognize interactions among society, the environment and
      technology, and understand technology's relationship with history.
      Consideration of these concepts forms a foundation for engaging in
      responsible and ethical use of technology.
       
    3. Technology for Productivity Applications
      Students learn the operations of technology through the usage of technology
      and productivity tools.
       
    4. Technology and Communication Applications
      Students use an array of technologies and apply design concepts to communicate with multiple audiences, acquire and disseminate information and enhance learning.
       
    5. Technology and Information Literacy
      Students engage in information literacy strategies, use the Internet, technology tools and resources, and apply information-management skills to answer questions and expand knowledge.
       
    6. Design
      Students apply a number of problem-solving strategies demonstrating the nature of design, the role of engineering and the role of assessment.
       
    7. Designed World
      Students understand how the physical, informational and bio-related technological systems of the designed world are brought about by the design process. Critical to this will be students' understanding of their role in the designed world: its processes, products, standards, services, history, future, impact, issues and career connections.
       
  • 七個課程標準之下，依年段 (gradebands) 設定不同程度的評鑑基準 (benchmarks)，每個年級亦有更細的能力指標 (indicators)
    • 四個年段為：
      • Grades K-2 (相當於臺灣的幼稚園到小二)
      • Grades 3-5 (相當於臺灣的小三到小五)
      • Grades 6-8 (相當於臺灣的小六到國二)
      • Grades 9-12 (相當於臺灣的國三到高三)
         
  • 由於中小學資訊課給人的「刻板印象」，就是教學生認識電腦與使用電腦，所以我就「故意」先從「認識電腦」談起。
     
  • 以下與「認識電腦」相關的標準、評鑑基準、能力指標之翻譯來自俄亥俄州科技課程標準全文PDF檔的第91頁到第97頁
     
  • Standard 3: Technology for Productivity Applications
    標準三：以科技為生產資訊的應用工具

    （明勳注：標準三之下有11個評鑑基準，k-2 三個、3-5 三個、6-8 三個、9-12兩個，在此僅列出與「認識電腦」有關的）
     
  • Benchmarks by Gradebands 年段評鑑基準
    • Grades K-2 Benchmark A:
      • Understand basic computer and multimedia technology concepts and terminology.
        能瞭解基本的電腦和多媒體科技的概念和術語。
    • Grades 3-5 Benchmark A:
      • Understand computer and multimedia technology concepts and communicate using the correct terminology.
        能瞭解電腦和多媒體科技的概念，並能使用正確的術語與人溝通。
    • Grades 6-8 Benchmark A:
      • Demonstrate an understanding of concepts underlying hardware, software and connectivity.
        能展現對電腦軟硬體和網路連結的瞭解。
         
  • Indicators by Grade Levels 分年能力指標
    （明勳注：僅列舉與「認識電腦」有關的）
    • Kindergarten 幼稚園
      1. Locate computer and multimedia technology in the classroom and
        identify it by name (e.g., computer, VCR, listening station).
        能指出教室裡哪裡有電腦和多媒體科技，並能用它們的名稱指稱之。（例如：電腦、錄放影機、listening station 等）
      2. Name the basic parts of a computer (e.g., monitor, keyboard, mouse,
        printer).
        能說出電腦基本元件的名稱（例如：螢幕、鍵盤、滑鼠、印表機等）
      3. Use computer and multimedia technology with teacher assistance (e.g.,
        computer, VCR, listening station).
        能在老師的協助下使用電腦和多媒體科技。
    • Grade One 一年級
      1. Identify and use computer and multimedia technology and know the
        terms used to describe it (e.g., computer, printer, VCR, DVD player,
        audio players).
        對電腦和多媒體科技，能指出、使用、說出名稱
      2. Identify various parts of a computer by name (e.g., monitor, mouse, keyboard, power button, disk drive, CD/DVD drive).
        能說出多種電腦元件的名稱（例如：螢幕、鍵盤、滑鼠、電源開關、磁碟機、光碟機等）
    • Grade Two 二年級
      1. Identify and describe the purpose of various types of computer and
        multimedia technology (e.g., what is it and what does it do?).
        能指認和描述多種類型的電腦和多媒體科技的用途。
      2. Use correct terminology when talking about computers and multimedia
        technology.
        在與人談論電腦和多媒體科技時，能使用正確的術語。
      3. Know that software is necessary to operate computer technology.
        能知道軟體是操作電腦所必需的。
      4. Use a variety of computer and multimedia technology resources for
        directed learning activities (e.g., computer, VCR/DVD player, audio
        player, camera).
        能在老師安排的學習活動中使用多種電腦和多媒體科技資源。
    • Grade Three 三年級
      1. Discuss the purpose of various types of computer and multimedia
        technology equipment using appropriate terminology.
        能用適當的術語與人討論多種類型的電腦和多媒體設備的用途。
      2. Communicate about computers and multimedia technology using
        correct terminology.
        能用正確的電腦和多媒體科技術語與人溝通。
    • Grade Four 四年級
      1. Learn and use new technology terminology based on the computer and
        multimedia technology resources being used.
        能學會和使用關於正在使用的電腦和多媒體資源之新的（以前沒學過的）科技術語。
      2. Define technological terms as discovered.
        能定義所新認識的科技名詞。
    • Grade Five 五年級
      1. Define and use new technology terminology based on the computer and
        multimedia technology resources being used.
        能定義和使用關於正在使用的電腦和多媒體資源之新的（以前沒學過的）科技術語。
    • Grade Six 六年級
      1. Use vocabulary related to computer and multimedia technology systems
        (e.g., network, local area network—LAN, wide area network—WAN,
        wireless, connectivity).
        能使用電腦和多媒體系統相關的字彙。（例如：網路、區域網路、廣域網路滑鼠、網路連線等）
      2. Describe how computers connect to the Internet (e.g., what is the
        information superhighway/World Wide Web and how can you connect
        to it?).
        能描述電腦如何連接到網際網路（例如：什麼是資訊高速公路、全球資訊網、你如何連上去？等）
    • Grade Seven 七年級
      1. Use vocabulary related to computer and multimedia technology systems
        (e.g., universal serial bus—USB, hubs and switches).
        能使用電腦和多媒體系統相關的字彙。（例如：USB、集線器、交換器等）
      2. Explain how computer components interact.
        能解釋電腦各部份零件的功用。
      3. Explain the purpose and different functions of software programs.
        能解釋電腦軟體程式的用途和不同功能。
    • Grade Eight 八年級
      1. Describe how computer and multimedia technology systems work (e.g.,
        asynchronous transfer mode—ATM, Internet protocol—IP, local area
        networks—LAN, wide area networks—WAN, wireless).
        能描述電腦和多媒體科技系統是如何運作的。（例如：ATM、IP、LAN、WAN等）
