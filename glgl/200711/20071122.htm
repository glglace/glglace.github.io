  • 11月14日下午在草屯國小計惠卿教授的演講中，簡報中第九個投影片提到美國教育部的一個研究報告顯示：美國每年花二十億美金買教學軟體，可是並無助於學習成效，資料來源是 2007.04.05 Washington Post的一篇報導，Software's Benefits On Tests In Doubt --- Study Says Tools Don't Raise Scores。而這篇報導是根據美國聯邦政府教育部2007年3月公佈的一份研究報告Effectiveness of Reading and Mathematics Software Products Findings from the First Student Cohort - Summary of Study Finding。
    　
  • 這份研究報告標明是屬於「公眾領域public domain」，意即任何人都可以自由地重製、散佈以其他方式利用之。惟需註明來源為：
    • Dynarski, Mark, Roberto Agodini, Sheila Heaviside, Timothy Novak, Nancy Carey, Larissa Campuzano, Barbara Means, Robert Murphy, William Penuel, Hal Javitz, Deborah Emery, and Willow Sussex. Effectiveness of Reading and Mathematics Software Products: Findings from the First Student Cohort, Washington, D.C.: U.S. Department of Education, Institute of Education
      Sciences, 2007.

    • 標準化測驗的成績能代表學生的學習成就嗎？

    • PDF全文下載 （共140頁）
    • 研究計畫名稱
      • Effectiveness of Reading and Mathematics Software Products: Findings from the First Student Cohort
    • 第16頁
      • Educational technology is used for word processing, presentation, spreadsheets, databases, internet search, distance education, virtual schools, interactions with simulations and models, and collaboration over local and global networks.
        教育科技是被用來做文書處理、簡報、試算表、資料庫、網路搜尋、遠距教學、虛擬學校、與模擬和模型互動，還有網路合作。
        （這個說法我不太能接受，一點教育味兒都沒有。）
      • Technology also is used as assistive devices for students with disabilities and to teach concepts or skills that are difficult or impossible to convey without technology.
        科技通常也被用為身心障礙學生的學習輔具，以及用來教如果沒有科技就難以傳達的概念或技巧。
      • This study is specifically focused on whether students had higher reading or math test scores when teachers had access to selected software products designed to support learning in reading or mathematics.
        這個研究是特別聚焦在，當老師有選擇並使用可以幫助閱讀或數學學習的教學軟體時，學生的閱讀或數學測驗成績是否會比較高？
      • It was not designed to assess the effectiveness of educational technology across its entire spectrum of uses, and the study's findings do not support conclusions about technology's effectiveness beyond the study's context, such as in other subject areas.
        這個研究不是設計來評定所有教育科技的有效性，而且此研究之發現（閱讀和數學方面）並不能用來推論到其他學習領域。
        　
    • 第15頁
      • The main findings of the study are: 研究的主要發現有兩點：
        1. Test Scores Were Not Significantly Higher in Classrooms Using Selected Reading and Mathematics Software Products.
          Test scores in treatment classrooms that were randomly assigned to use products did not differ from test scores in control classrooms by statistically significant margins.
          　
        2. Effects Were Correlated With Some Classroom and School Characteristics.
          For reading products, effects on overall test scores were correlated with the student-teacher ratio in first grade classrooms and with the amount of time that products were used in fourth grade classrooms. For math products, effects were uncorrelated with classroom and school characteristics.
